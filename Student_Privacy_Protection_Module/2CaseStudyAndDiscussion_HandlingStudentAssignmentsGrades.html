If possible, comments should not be compiled on the front or back of assignments. This makes them visible to other students when the assignments are returned.

You just finished marking the homework sets, where should you write the final grade?

 
On the back of the front page
Best Practice

On the front of the package
Poor Practice
 
On the front of the back page
Best Practice

On the back of the package
Poor Practice
 
Unless instructed to do so, avoid writing the grades on the front or back of assignments. Grades are confidential to everyone but the student and to the University (abeit on a need to know basis).

You are ready to return the assignments to your students, how should you do so?

 
Let the students pick up their assignments from an alphabetized pile at the front of the room
Poor Practice

Ask students to provide their name to pick up packages from you
Best Practice

Allow students to pick up packages from you by providing their names and the names of their friends
Poor Practice

Allow piles of packages to circulate through the student crowd until everyone present has picked up their package
Poor Practice
 
Grades are confidential to everyone but the student and to the University (abeit on a need to know basis). You should return work in a controlled and supervised manner and you cannot allow students to collect the work of others. Also, you should reliably retain unclaimed student work for one year.
