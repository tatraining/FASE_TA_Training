The University of Toronto is covered by Ontario's Freedom of Information and Privacy Protection Act (FIPPA).

This Act extends to TAing and covers the handling of:

  1. Assignments and grades
  2. Communication with students
  3. Keeping records, and disclosure

 
 

FIPPA covers the following as personal information that should be protected

 


Email Adresses


Grades


Names


ID Numbers

Academic Records
and References
 
 

While completing this module, and throughout your TA experience, keep this in mind:

  1. Emergencies, health (mental and physical), and personal safety always trump student privacy
  2. Although personal information can be shared within U of T, it should only be done so on a need-to-know basis and/or with the consent of the student
