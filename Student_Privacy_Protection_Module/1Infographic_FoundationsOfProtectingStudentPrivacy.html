The University of Toronto is covered by Ontario's Freedom of Information and Privacy Protection Act (FIPPA).
This Act extends to TAing and covers the handling of assignments and grades, communication with students, keeping records, and disclosure.

 
 

FIPPA covers the following as personal information that should be protected

 

Email Adresses

Grades

Names

ID Numbers
