The following is a list of ideas/strategies you can use to humanize your session and gain the trust of your students ...

 
 


Make an Introduction Video

Create a short video (about 1 min) introducing yourself. Include personal aspects (e.g. hobbies, experiences in the course, etc.) if you are comfortable doing so.

 


Take Advantage of UofT Time

Begin the virtual conference on time and convert the 10 min delay into an open discussion/small-talk period. If you want to ask your students about their personal wellbeing, keep it general and open. This allows the students to determine how much they want to share without feeling pressured.

 


Show Your Face!

Whenever possible, keep your video feed online. You can also upload a picture of yourself if the conferencing platform (e.g. BB Collaborate in Quercus, Microsoft Teams, etc.) allows it.
