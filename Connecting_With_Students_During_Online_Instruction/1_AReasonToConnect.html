In traditionally online courses, instructor-student communication has been found to be incredibly beneficial to students

These benefits include:



Helping students keep
pace with the course


Increasing student resilience
when faced with roadblocks


Improving levels of perceived
learning by students in the course


Raising overall student
satisfaction with the course
 

In order to have sufficient instructor-student to reap these benefits, you as a TA, need to make a personal connection with your student. You need to gain their trust.
