As a TA, having a close personal relationship with a student that you are responsible for assesisng creates a conflict of interest

A person with whom you have close personal relationahips may be a:



Family Member


Friend


Colleague

Romantic or
Sexual Partner
 
      Did you know: You are not allowed to tutor a student for money if they are in the course you are TAing. Doing so is as a prohibited conflict of interest
 
 

If a conflict of interest exists at the start or develops during a course, you MUST REPORT IT to your course instructor. Furthermore, you must not be responsible for any portion of the student's grade, all grading for that student must be transferred to another TA.
