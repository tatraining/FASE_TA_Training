Duties regarding academic offences must be addressed in the TA contract and be allocated hours. These duties may include, but are not limited to, confirming evidence of plagiarism, writing a report about behaviour witnessed at a test or exam, and assisting with instructor-student interviews (e.g. taking notes during the meeting). Note however that it is the responsibility of the course instructor to conduct these interviews with the student.

Course instructors nor TAs have the authority to make sanctions regarding academic offences. When an assignment is worth 10% or less of the final mark, the Department Chair may impose sanction or refer the case to the Dean. The Dean can impose sanctions on any assesment/test regarless of its weighting.
