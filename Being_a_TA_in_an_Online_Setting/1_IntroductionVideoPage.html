Creating an introduction video or paragraph about yourself allows you to connect with your students and let them know that you are “in this together.” It gives a little extra piece of who you are and puts a face and voice to things.

You can record yourself, or create a page like this. Once you have created your video or document, share it in an announcement or discussion post!
