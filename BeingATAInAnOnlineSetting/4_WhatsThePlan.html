If This is the First Tutorial:

Consider showing students the kinds of problems they will be able to solve or discuss at the end of the course. You can begin by discussing some of the current issues, controversies, or innovations in the discipline. Show your students how the course content will help them answer many of the questions that they might have.

 
 
At the Beginning of Every Tutorial:

Share the learning outcomes you have decided to focus on during this tutorial. Indicate what the overall goal is for that day.

Indicate what activities you’ll be doing and what exercises/problems you’ll be working on, as well as how much time you’ll be allotting each part of the class. It is sometimes helpful to put an outline for that day’s class in the chat.
