When preparing to facilitate a tutorial or lab/practical,  speak with the course instructor (CI) before hand to discuss tutorial expectations, policies and schedule.

Consider Asking:

What will the tutorial or lab sections be used for and how do they relate to the lecture portion of the class?

Will you be expected to lead the tutorial or lab sections?

  • If so, which tutorial or lab section or sections will you lead?
  • If so, who will be in charge of preparing the tutorial or lab section?

How far in advance of the actual tutorial or lab section will you receive information about what should be in it?
